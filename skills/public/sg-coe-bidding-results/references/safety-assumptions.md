# Safety Assumptions: SG COE Bidding Results

- The skill is informational and does not replace professional financial or legal advice.
- Official LTA/OneMotoring data is the ground truth for exercise results.
- The skill does not fabricate prices, quotas, demand metrics, or exercise status.
- Future-bid guarantees are out of scope and must be rejected or reframed with uncertainty.
- Personalized certainty claims that may cause financial harm require human escalation.
