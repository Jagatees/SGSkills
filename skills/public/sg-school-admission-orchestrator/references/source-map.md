# Source Map (Singapore School Admissions)

Prioritize sources in this order:

1. MOE official pages and directories
- School information pages
- Admissions eligibility/process pages
- Official announcements and timeline pages

2. Individual school official websites
- Program offerings
- CCA and support services
- Open house and admissions notes

3. Government/statutory education-related pages when needed
- Supporting policy context only when directly relevant

Rules:
- Prefer primary official sources over secondary summaries.
- If a fact is not verifiable from an official source, mark confidence `Medium` at most.
- If date or policy status is unclear, move the item to caveats/watchlist.
