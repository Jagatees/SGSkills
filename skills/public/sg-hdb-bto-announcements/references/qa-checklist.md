# QA Checklist

- Scope captured or defaulted (launch + process).
- Output uses simple, step-based language.
- All major claims have official source links.
- Dates are absolute (`Day Month Year`).
- Facts and interpretation are clearly separated.
- No promises on ballot success, queue position, or financing approval.
- Confidence labels included in each major section.
- Caveat included when information may change.
