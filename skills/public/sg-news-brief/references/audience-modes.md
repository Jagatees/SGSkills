# Audience Modes

Use these defaults to tune the same news set for different readers.

## Public readers

- Tone: neutral and plain.
- Focus: what happened, who is affected, what changes next.
- Avoid: heavy jargon and finance shorthand.

## Founders and SMEs

- Tone: practical and action-oriented.
- Focus: compliance, customer demand shifts, labor and cost impact.
- Add: "what to do this week" bullets where relevant.

## Investors

- Tone: concise and signal-focused.
- Focus: policy direction, sector exposure, earnings/revenue implications.
- Add: upside/downside framing with uncertainty notes.

## Students

- Tone: educational and simple.
- Focus: background context and key terms before analysis.
- Add: short glossary lines when technical terms appear.

## Internal teams

- Tone: operational and decision-ready.
- Focus: risk level, urgency, owner, and recommended action.
- Add: 3-point action summary at end.
