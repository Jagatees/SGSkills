# Source Priority (Singapore News Brief)

Use this priority order when selecting sources.

## Tier 1: Primary Singapore sources

- Singapore government and statutory board websites (official announcements, advisories, policy updates)
- Parliament and ministry statements
- MAS, MOM, MOH, LTA, HDB, CPF Board, IRAS, ACRA notices

Use these first for policy, regulation, and official figures.

## Tier 2: Established Singapore newsrooms

- The Straits Times
- CNA
- TODAY
- The Business Times

Use for broad daily coverage and fast updates. Cross-check major claims with primary sources when possible.

## Tier 3: Regional and global outlets

- Reuters
- Bloomberg
- Financial Times
- AP

Use only when they materially add context to Singapore impact (markets, geopolitics, multinationals).

## Exclusions and caution

- Avoid unverified social media claims as primary evidence.
- Avoid repeating rumors unless clearly labeled as unconfirmed.
- Prefer direct links to article pages or official statements, not aggregator reposts.
