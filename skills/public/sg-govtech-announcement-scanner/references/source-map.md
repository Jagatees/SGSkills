# Source Map

Use these source tiers in order.

## Tier 1 (preferred)

- GovTech official pages (updates, status, announcements)
- OGP official pages and product updates
- Official agency announcement/release pages

## Tier 2

- Statutory board newsrooms
- Official consultation and circular pages

## Tier 3 (only when needed)

- Reputable outlets clearly citing Tier 1/2 sources

## Exclusion rule

Exclude any item that lacks:
- a clear publish/update date, or
- a verifiable source URL.
