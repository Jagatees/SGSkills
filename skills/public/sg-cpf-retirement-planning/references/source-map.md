## Not Trusted Sources

**Do NOT cite as authoritative**

| Source Type | Reason |
|-------------|--------|
| Non-.gov.sg websites | Not official government source |
| Personal blogs | Unverified information |
| Social media | Unreliable, may be outdated |
| Commercial financial sites | May have conflicts of interest |
| Forums (Reddit, HardwareZone) | User-generated, unverified |

**Note**: While commercial financial sites (banks, fintech blogs) may provide helpful explanations, always verify numbers against official CPF sources and cite CPF as the authoritative source.

---

## Source Verification Checklist

Before citing any source:

- [ ] Is domain .gov.sg?
- [ ] Is the page currently accessible?
- [ ] Is the information dated within the relevant period?
- [ ] Is this the official source for this data type?
- [ ] Have I checked for more recent updates?

## Source Citation Format

### In Response
```
OA interest rate: 2.5% p.a. (floor rate)
Source: cpf.gov.sg/service/article/what-are-the-cpf-interest-rates
Confidence: High
```

### Full Citation
```
Central Provident Fund Board. "What are the CPF interest rates?"
https://www.cpf.gov.sg/service/article/what-are-the-cpf-interest-rates
(Accessed: 15 January 2026)
```
