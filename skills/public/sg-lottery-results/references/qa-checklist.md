# QA Checklist: SG Lottery Results

- [ ] `As of` timestamp is present in SGT.
- [ ] Scope (4D/TOTO/both and latest/specific date) is explicit.
- [ ] Official Singapore Pools source is used for result claims.
- [ ] Each requested product includes draw date and source link.
- [ ] Winning numbers are copied from official source or marked pending.
- [ ] Confidence labels are attached to major sections.
- [ ] Facts and inference are separated.
- [ ] No betting-advice or guaranteed-win language appears.
- [ ] Publication status is assessed and stated.
- [ ] Publication status basis is explicit (official results-page presence/absence, or cited official schedule/announcement).
- [ ] Verification pending note is present when publication cannot be confirmed.
