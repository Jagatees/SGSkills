# Source Map: SG Lottery Results

## Tier 1 (primary)
- Singapore Pools official 4D results pages
- Singapore Pools official TOTO results pages

## Tier 2
- Official Singapore Pools announcements on draw schedules, publication delays, and exceptions

## Tier 3 (supporting only)
- Media summaries or community reposts of result numbers

## Rules
- Never issue winning-number claims from Tier 3 alone.
- Prefer official Singapore Pools pages when sources differ.
- Include direct links and absolute timestamps for major sections.
- If publication status is unclear or pending, lower confidence and state limitations.
