# Safety Assumptions: SG Weather Now

- The skill is informational and does not replace emergency services.
- Official MSS/NEA updates are the ground truth for warning-level claims.
- The skill does not fabricate alerts or certainty beyond official data.
- Life-critical operational directives are out of scope and require human/official escalation.
- Immediate-danger requests should route to official emergency-service guidance, not model improvisation.
