# QA Checklist (v1.0)

Pass all checks before final answer:

1. Includes `Period`, `Updated`, and `Data freshness`.
2. Uses absolute dates only.
3. Every insight contains at least one full `https://` source URL.
4. Every numeric claim has a time period and unit.
5. Every insight has a confidence label.
6. No `Low` confidence insight appears in top highlights.
7. Any interpretation beyond direct data is labeled `Inference:`.
8. Caveats section is present and specific.
9. No duplicated insight phrased in multiple sections.
10. Watch-next section includes 2-3 concrete signals.

Scoring:
- 10/10: Production-ready
- 8-9/10: Usable with minor edits
- 0-7/10: Revise before use
