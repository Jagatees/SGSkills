# Source Map

Use these source tiers in order.

## Tier 1 (preferred)

- Land Transport Authority (LTA) official advisories and announcements
- SMRT official service update pages and channels
- SBS Transit official service update pages and channels
- Other official operator pages (Tower Transit, Go-Ahead Singapore)

## Tier 2

- Operator newsroom posts clarifying ongoing disruptions or recovery windows
- Relevant government transport notices

## Tier 3 (only when needed)

- Reputable news outlets that explicitly cite Tier 1/Tier 2 updates

## Exclusion rule

Exclude any item without:
- a clear publish/update timestamp, or
- a verifiable source URL.
